# How to Download Multiple Orders as a ZIP File

Bulk download lets you download multiple order invoices at once. For smaller batches, you receive a ZIP file directly. For larger batches, the file is sent to your email.

## Prerequisites

- Better PDF Invoice app installed on your Shopify store
- Two or more orders synced from your Shopify store

## Steps

### Step 1: Open the Orders Page

Navigate to **Orders** in the left sidebar to view your order list.

{/* screenshot: Orders page with the order table */}

### Step 2: Select Multiple Orders

Check the checkboxes next to each order you want to download. You can select orders individually or use the header checkbox to select all visible orders.

{/* screenshot: Multiple orders selected with checkboxes */}

### Step 3: Click Download Selected Orders

In the bulk action bar that appears, click **Download selected orders**. The app begins generating the ZIP file.

{/* screenshot: Bulk action bar with Download selected orders button */}

### Step 4: Wait for the Download

A "Generating..." loading indicator appears while the app compiles the invoices. For batches of 30 or fewer orders, the ZIP file downloads directly to your browser.

{/* screenshot: Generating spinner while download is being prepared */}

### Step 5: Check Your Email for Larger Batches

If you selected more than 15 orders, a message appears: "We started to send an email to your email with an attachment that contains all selected orders. You will receive it shortly." Check your inbox for the email with the ZIP attachment.

{/* screenshot: Message confirming the email with attachment is being sent */}

### Step 6: Download All Orders (Cross-Page Selection)

For Professional plan users, after selecting orders you can use the **Total Selected** banner that appears above the table. This shows the total count of selected orders across pages with a **Details** dropdown. Click **Download All** to download every selected order, or click **Download all orders** in the bulk action bar to download all orders matching your current filters.

{/* screenshot: Total Selected banner with Details dropdown and Download All button */}

### Step 7: Verify the Downloaded File

Open the downloaded ZIP file from your browser's download folder. It contains individual PDF invoices for each selected order.

{/* screenshot: Downloaded ZIP file contents showing individual PDF files */}

### Step 8: Download Historical Orders (Wholesale Plan)

If you are on the Wholesale plan, a **Download historical** button appears in the page header as a secondary action. Click it to open a modal where you can select a template and language, then download historical orders in bulk.

{/* screenshot: Download historical modal with template and language selection */}

## Tips

- The "Download all orders" bulk action requires a Professional plan. A "Professional" badge appears next to the button if you need to upgrade.
- For batches over 30 orders, the app processes the download in the background and emails the result to you.
- You can remove individual orders from a cross-page selection by clicking the X button next to the order name in the **Details** popover.
- Use the **Clear** button in the Total Selected banner to deselect all orders across pages.
