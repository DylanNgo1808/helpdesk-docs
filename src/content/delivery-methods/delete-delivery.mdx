# How to Remove a Delivery Method

If you no longer need a delivery method, you can permanently delete it. This stops all future file exports through that delivery and removes it from your delivery list.

## Prerequisites

- An existing delivery method that you want to remove
- A paid plan (Professional, Ultimate, or Wholesale)

## Steps

### Step 1: Navigate to Settings

From the Avada PDF Invoice dashboard, click **Settings** in the left sidebar menu, then select the **Delivery** tab.

{/* screenshot: Settings page with Delivery tab selected */}

### Step 2: Open the Delivery Method

Click on the name of the delivery method you want to delete. This opens the delivery detail page.

{/* screenshot: Delivery list with a delivery name highlighted */}

### Step 3: Scroll to the Page Actions

Scroll down to the bottom of the delivery page. You will see the **Save** and **Delete** buttons in the page actions area.

{/* screenshot: Bottom of delivery page showing Save and Delete buttons */}

### Step 4: Click Delete

Click the **Delete** button. This is styled as a destructive (red) action to indicate the operation is permanent.

{/* screenshot: Delete button highlighted in red */}

### Step 5: Confirm the Deletion

A confirmation modal will appear asking you to verify that you want to delete this delivery. The modal displays the delivery name so you can confirm you are deleting the correct one.

{/* screenshot: Confirm delete delivery modal with delivery name */}

### Step 6: Verify the Deletion

After confirming, you will be redirected to the delivery list under **Settings** > **Delivery** tab. A "Delivery deleted" toast notification confirms the deletion was successful.

{/* screenshot: Delivery list after deletion with success toast */}

## Tips

- Deleting a delivery method is permanent and cannot be undone. If you want to temporarily stop exports, consider disabling the delivery by clicking **Turn off** instead.
- Make sure no critical workflows depend on the delivery before removing it.
- You can always create a new delivery method later if you need to restore file exports to the same server.
