# How to Manage B2B Companies

The B2B Companies page lets you view all companies associated with your Shopify B2B setup. You can search, sort, and navigate to individual company details from this central list.

## Prerequisites

- A Wholesale plan in Better PDF Invoice
- B2B companies set up in your Shopify admin
- B2B access scopes granted to the app (you may be prompted to grant access on first visit)

## Steps

### Step 1: Navigate to B2B

From the Better PDF Invoice dashboard, click **B2B** in the left sidebar menu. This opens the **B2B Purchases** page.

{/* screenshot: Left sidebar with B2B menu item highlighted */}

### Step 2: Grant B2B Access Scopes (If Prompted)

If this is your first time visiting the B2B page, a warning banner may appear asking you to grant additional access scopes. Click **Grant access** to authorize the app to read your B2B company data. You can also click **Status scopes** to view which scopes are granted and which are required.

{/* screenshot: Warning banner with Grant access button and Status scopes link */}

### Step 3: View the Company List

The company index table displays all your B2B companies with the following columns:
- **Company** - the company name (clickable to view details)
- **Locations** - number of company locations
- **Main contact** - the primary contact person (or "No main contact" if none is set)
- **Total orders** - number of orders placed by the company
- **Total sales** - total revenue from the company

{/* screenshot: Company index table with column headings */}

### Step 4: Search for a Company

Use the search bar above the table to filter companies by name. Type a company name and the list filters in real time.

{/* screenshot: Search bar with a company name typed in */}

### Step 5: Sort the Company List

Click the sort options to reorder companies. Available sort options include:
- **Name** - A-Z or Z-A
- **Orders** - Low to High or High to Low
- **Sales** - Low to High or High to Low

{/* screenshot: Sort dropdown showing available sort options */}

### Step 6: Navigate Between Pages

If you have more than 50 companies, use the pagination controls at the bottom of the table to navigate between pages.

{/* screenshot: Pagination controls at bottom of company list */}

### Step 7: Open a Company

Click any company name in the list to navigate to that company's detail page, where you can view orders, contacts, and statistics.

{/* screenshot: Company name link in the table row */}

## Tips

- The B2B feature requires the Wholesale plan. If you are on a lower plan, you will see a lock icon and an **Upgrade now** button prompting you to upgrade.
- Company data is synced from your Shopify B2B settings. To add or remove companies, manage them in your Shopify admin.
- The search filters companies by name only. Use the company detail page for more advanced order filtering.
