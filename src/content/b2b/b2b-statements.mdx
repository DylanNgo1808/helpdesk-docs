# How to Create B2B Statements

B2B statements are combined PDF documents that summarize orders for a specific company over a given time period. You can generate statements for all orders, outstanding (unpaid/partial) orders only, or a custom date range.

## Prerequisites

- A Wholesale plan in Better PDF Invoice
- B2B companies with orders in your Shopify admin
- B2B access scopes granted to the app

## Steps

### Step 1: Navigate to a Company

From the Better PDF Invoice dashboard, click **B2B** in the sidebar, then click on the company you want to generate a statement for.

{/* screenshot: B2B company list with a company highlighted */}

### Step 2: Open the Statement Modal

On the company detail page, click the **Download statement** button. This opens a modal where you can configure your statement options.

{/* screenshot: Download statement button on the company detail page */}

### Step 3: Choose a Filter Option

In the statement modal, select one of the following filter options:
- **All orders** - includes all orders from the company in the current date range
- **Outstanding orders only (unpaid/partial)** - includes only orders that have not been fully paid
- **Custom** - allows you to specify a custom date range

{/* screenshot: Statement modal with three filter options displayed */}

### Step 4: Set a Custom Date Range (Optional)

If you selected **Custom**, two date picker fields appear:
- **Start date** - the beginning of the statement period
- **End date** - the end of the statement period

A warning banner reminds you that statements are limited to a 90-day rolling period. If your selected range exceeds 90 days, an error message will appear and the **Download** button will be disabled.

{/* screenshot: Custom date range fields with 90-day warning banner */}

### Step 5: Download the Statement

Click the **Download** button in the modal. The app generates a PDF statement file and automatically downloads it to your computer. The file is named using the pattern: `statement_[company_name]_[start_date]_[end_date].pdf`.

{/* screenshot: Download button in statement modal */}

### Step 6: Cancel If Needed

If you change your mind, click **Cancel** or close the modal to return to the company detail page without generating a statement.

{/* screenshot: Cancel button in the modal */}

### Step 7: Verify the Download

After the download completes, a success toast notification confirms the statement was generated. Open the downloaded PDF to verify it contains the correct orders and date range.

{/* screenshot: Success toast notification after statement download */}

## Tips

- The **Outstanding orders only** filter is useful for sending payment reminders to B2B customers with unpaid balances.
- Statements are limited to a 90-day date range. If you need a longer period, generate multiple statements covering different date ranges.
- The statement PDF includes order details, payment status, and totals for the selected period.
- If no orders are found in the selected custom date range, a notification will alert you that no orders were found.
- The custom filter suffix is added to the filename (e.g., `_outstanding`) to help you distinguish between different statement types.
