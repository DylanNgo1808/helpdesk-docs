# How to View Company Details and Contacts

When you open a B2B company from the company list, you can view detailed statistics, order history, and contact information. This page serves as the central hub for managing all interactions with a specific B2B customer.

## Prerequisites

- A Wholesale plan in Better PDF Invoice
- B2B companies and orders set up in your Shopify admin
- B2B access scopes granted to the app

## Steps

### Step 1: Navigate to the Company List

From the Better PDF Invoice dashboard, click **B2B** in the left sidebar menu to open the **B2B Purchases** page.

{/* screenshot: B2B Purchases page with company list */}

### Step 2: Select a Company

Click on a company name in the list. This navigates you to the company's detail page showing orders and statistics specific to that company.

{/* screenshot: Company name being clicked in the list */}

### Step 3: View the Statistics Bar

At the top of the company detail page, a statistics card displays key financial metrics:
- **Total order value** - the total value of all orders from this company
- **Total amount paid** - the amount already paid
- **Total amount owed** - the outstanding balance
- **Total amount past due** - overdue payments

{/* screenshot: Statistics bar showing the four financial metrics */}

### Step 4: Change the Date Range

Click the date range selector on the left side of the statistics bar. Choose between:
- **This month** - compared to the previous month
- **Last 30 days** - compared to the previous 30 days

The statistics and order list update based on your selected date range.

{/* screenshot: Date range popover with This month and Last 30 days options */}

### Step 5: View Customer Information

In the order table, click on a customer name to open a popover with their contact details. This popover shows customer information and allows you to view their associated orders.

{/* screenshot: Customer popover showing contact details */}

### Step 6: Browse Company Orders

The order index table shows all orders from the selected company with columns for order name, date, customer, financial status, fulfillment status, and total price. Each order name links to the full order detail view.

{/* screenshot: Order index table showing company orders */}

### Step 7: Navigate Back to the Company List

Click the back arrow at the top of the page to return to the main B2B company list.

{/* screenshot: Back navigation arrow at top of company detail page */}

## Tips

- The statistics bar provides a quick financial overview for each company. Use it to identify companies with outstanding or overdue balances.
- All financial amounts are displayed in your store's currency.
- Company and contact data is synced from Shopify. Update company details in your Shopify admin to see changes reflected in the app.
