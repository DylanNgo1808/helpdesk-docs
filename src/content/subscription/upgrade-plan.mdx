# How to Upgrade to a Higher Plan

Upgrading your plan unlocks additional features like unlimited orders, custom email senders, multiple currencies, B2B management, and more. You can upgrade at any time, and Shopify will prorate the charge.

## Prerequisites

- An active Avada PDF Invoice installation
- A Shopify store with billing enabled
- For the Wholesale plan: a Shopify Plus store

## Steps

### Step 1: Navigate to the Subscription Page

From the Avada PDF Invoice dashboard, click **Subscription** in the left sidebar menu.

{/* screenshot: Left sidebar with Subscription highlighted */}

### Step 2: Choose Your Billing Interval

At the top of the page, select either **Monthly** or **Annually**. Choosing the annual billing interval saves you 18% compared to monthly billing. The **Save 18%** badge highlights this discount.

{/* screenshot: Monthly and Annually toggle with Save 18% badge */}

### Step 3: Review the Available Plans

Compare the plan cards to identify which plan has the features you need:
- **Professional** - customizable templates, bulk printing, unlimited orders, POS integration
- **Ultimate** - multiple currencies, priority support, expert template customization
- **Wholesale** - B2B company management, net terms, balance tracking (Shopify Plus only)

{/* screenshot: Three plan cards displayed side by side */}

### Step 4: Select Your New Plan

Click the action button on the plan card you want to upgrade to. The button label varies:
- **Start 7-day free trial** - if you have not used a trial for this plan
- **Select plan** - if you have already used a trial
- **Upgrade to Shopify Plus** - shown on Wholesale for non-Shopify Plus stores

{/* screenshot: Plan card with Select plan / Start trial button */}

### Step 5: Approve the Charge in Shopify

After clicking the plan button, you are redirected to your Shopify admin to approve the subscription charge. Review the charge details (plan name, price, billing interval) and click **Approve** to confirm.

{/* screenshot: Shopify admin charge approval page */}

### Step 6: Verify the Upgrade

After approving, you are redirected back to the Avada PDF Invoice subscription page. A success banner reading **"Congratulations! You upgraded successfully!"** confirms the upgrade was applied.

{/* screenshot: Success banner on the subscription page */}

### Step 7: Confirm Your New Plan

Your new plan card now shows a **Current plan** label, and the Plan details section at the bottom updates with your new payment amount and next billing date.

{/* screenshot: Updated Plan details section with new plan information */}

## Tips

- When upgrading to a more expensive plan, Shopify prorates the charge within the same billing cycle. You only pay the difference for the remaining days.
- If you are upgrading from Free to a paid plan and have not used a trial, you get 7 days free on Professional and Ultimate plans.
- You can also reach the subscription page from feature-locked modals throughout the app. When you encounter a feature that requires a higher plan, clicking the upgrade prompt will take you to the subscription page.
- A discount code can be applied before upgrading to reduce the subscription cost. See [Applying discount codes](/docs/subscription/discount-codes).
