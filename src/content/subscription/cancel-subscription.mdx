# How to Cancel Your Subscription

If you no longer need paid features, you can downgrade from a paid plan to the Free plan. The downgrade process includes a feedback step and an optional discount offer to help you stay on a paid plan.

## Prerequisites

- An active paid subscription (Professional, Ultimate, or Wholesale) in Avada PDF Invoice

## Steps

### Step 1: Navigate to the Subscription Page

From the Avada PDF Invoice dashboard, click **Subscription** in the left sidebar menu.

{/* screenshot: Left sidebar with Subscription highlighted */}

### Step 2: Show the Free Plan

Click the **Show all plans** button below the pricing table to reveal the Free plan card.

{/* screenshot: Show all plans button below pricing table */}

### Step 3: Select the Free Plan

On the Free plan card, click the button to select it. This initiates the downgrade process.

{/* screenshot: Free plan card with downgrade button */}

### Step 4: Review the Downgrade Warning

A confirmation modal appears showing what features you will retain on the Free plan:
- 50 orders per month
- Multi currencies and languages
- English-only translations
- Send via Avada email
- All document types
- Automation email
- Tax exemptions

All features exclusive to your current paid plan will be disabled.

{/* screenshot: Downgrade confirmation modal showing remaining features */}

### Step 5: Select a Reason for Downgrading

From the dropdown menu, select the reason for your downgrade. Options include:
- Seeking a more affordable plan
- Decreased business size
- Found alternative tools with better pricing
- Did not see enough value to justify cost
- Only need advanced features occasionally
- Not using advanced features in current plan
- Wanting a less complex plan
- Experiencing problems with the current plan
- Plan no longer aligns with business goals
- Input suggests a lower-tier plan is suitable
- Other (with a text field for custom feedback)

{/* screenshot: Reason dropdown in the downgrade modal */}

### Step 6: Review the Discount Offer (If Available)

If you have not previously used a downgrade discount, a special offer modal appears offering **33% off for 2 months** on your current plan. You have two choices:
- **Take 33% discount** - stay on your current plan at a reduced rate
- **Continue downgrade** - proceed with switching to the Free plan

{/* screenshot: Discount offer modal showing 33% off for 2 months */}

### Step 7: Confirm the Downgrade

If you choose to continue the downgrade, click **Continue downgrade**. The app submits the plan change to Shopify, and your plan will switch to Free.

{/* screenshot: Continue downgrade button in the discount modal */}

### Step 8: Verify the Plan Change

The page refreshes after the downgrade is complete. Your subscription page now shows the Free plan as your current plan. Features exclusive to paid plans are no longer accessible.

{/* screenshot: Subscription page showing Free as the current plan */}

## Tips

- The Free plan limits you to 50 orders per month for downloading, printing, and sending invoices. Make sure this limit meets your needs before downgrading.
- Shopify subscription charges are billed at regular intervals (monthly or annually). If you downgrade before the billing period ends, you will still be charged for the full current period.
- The 33% discount offer is a one-time offer. If you have already used it, the discount modal will not appear.
- You can upgrade back to a paid plan at any time from the subscription page. No data is lost when you downgrade.
- If you want to completely uninstall the app instead of downgrading, note that Shopify will still charge you for the current billing period as per Shopify subscription policies.
