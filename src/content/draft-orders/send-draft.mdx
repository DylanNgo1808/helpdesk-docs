# How to Send a Draft Order Invoice by Email

You can send a draft order invoice (quote) directly to a customer by email from the draft order detail page. The send modal lets you specify the recipient, choose a template and language, and add BCC recipients.

## Prerequisites

- Better PDF Invoice app installed on your Shopify store
- A Professional or Ultimate plan
- A draft order with status other than "Completed"
- At least one Draft Order template configured
- The draft order should have a customer with an email address (optional but recommended)

## Steps

### Step 1: Open the draft order detail page

From the **Draft Orders** list, click on the draft order name to open its detail page.

{/* screenshot: Draft order detail page showing the order name and details */}

### Step 2: Click the Send Quote button

At the bottom of the **Payment** section, click the **Send Quote** button. This button is only visible when the draft order status is not "Completed."

{/* screenshot: Send Quote button at the bottom of the payment summary card */}

### Step 3: Enter the recipient email

In the send modal that opens, the **Send to** field is pre-filled with the customer's email address if one exists. You can modify this email or enter a different recipient address.

{/* screenshot: Send modal with the Send to email field pre-filled with customer email */}

### Step 4: Add BCC recipients (optional)

Use the **BCC Emails** field to add additional email recipients. Type an email address and select **Add "[email]"** from the dropdown to add it as a tag. You can add multiple BCC recipients. Click the "x" on any tag to remove it.

{/* screenshot: BCC Emails field with email tags added */}

### Step 5: Select an invoice template

Use the **Invoice template** dropdown to choose which Draft Order template to use for the PDF attachment. Only templates with the type "Draft Order" appear in this list.

{/* screenshot: Invoice template dropdown showing available Draft Order templates */}

### Step 6: Choose a language

Under the **Language** section, choose between:

- **Use billing language** (requires Ultimate plan) -- uses the language associated with the customer's billing profile. This option is disabled if the draft order has no customer.
- **Custom language** -- select a specific language from the dropdown.

{/* screenshot: Language choice list showing "Use billing language" and "Custom language" options */}

### Step 7: Send the invoice

Click the **Send** button at the bottom of the modal to send the draft order invoice email. The modal closes and the app sends the email with the PDF invoice attached.

{/* screenshot: Send button at the bottom of the send modal */}

### Step 8: Confirm the email was sent

After sending, a success toast notification appears confirming the email was sent. If there is an error, an error banner displays at the top of the page with the error message.

{/* screenshot: Success toast notification after sending the draft order invoice */}

## Tips

- The **Send Quote** button is hidden for draft orders with the "Completed" status, since completed drafts have already been converted to orders.
- If the draft order has no customer associated, the **Send to** field will be empty and you must enter an email address manually.
- The **Use billing language** option is available only on the Ultimate plan and requires the draft order to have an associated customer.
- BCC recipients receive a copy of the email without the primary recipient knowing.
- You can also send a draft order quote using email automation. See the email automation articles for automatic sending options.
