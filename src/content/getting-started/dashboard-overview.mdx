# How to Navigate the Dashboard

The Avada PDF Invoice dashboard is your central hub for managing invoices. This guide covers the main sections of the dashboard so you can quickly find what you need.

## Prerequisites

- Avada PDF Invoice installed on your Shopify store
- Access to your Shopify admin panel

## Steps

### Step 1: Open the App

In your Shopify admin, click **Apps** in the sidebar, then click **Avada PDF Invoice** to open the dashboard. You will land on the home page with a personalized greeting: "Hi [Your Name]!"

{/* screenshot: Shopify admin sidebar with "Apps" expanded and "Avada PDF Invoice" highlighted */}

### Step 2: Review the Report Cards

At the top of the dashboard, you will see two report cards: **Orders with downloaded invoices** (total number of orders for which invoices have been downloaded) and **Number of invoice emails sent** (total invoice emails sent from the app).

{/* screenshot: Dashboard showing the two report cards with download and email statistics */}

### Step 3: Explore the Quickstart Guide

Below the report cards, the **Quickstart** section displays a progress bar showing how many setup tasks you have completed (e.g., "2 of 4 tasks completed"). Each task has a button to take action.

{/* screenshot: Quickstart section showing the progress bar and the four setup tasks */}

### Step 4: Understand the Quickstart Tasks

The four Quickstart tasks are:

1. **Set up store's information and language** -- Configure your company name, address, email, and primary language.
2. **Select a PDF template** -- Choose your preferred PDF template for invoices.
3. **Customize your PDF template** -- Adjust the information displayed on your invoices.
4. **Print/Download your first Invoice** -- Print or download your latest invoice.

Click the action button on any incomplete task to begin.

{/* screenshot: Expanded Quickstart task showing the "Set up" button for store details */}

### Step 5: View Video Guides

Scroll down to find video guide cards covering topics like getting started, how D2C brands use the app, and B2B invoice features. Click **Learn more** on any card to open the guide. Click the menu icon and select **Dismiss** to hide a card.

{/* screenshot: Video guide cards showing "Welcome to AG Order Printer", "How D2C Brands Use Our App", and "Powerful B2B Invoice Features" */}

### Step 6: Browse Recommended Apps

Below the video guides, a **Handpicked apps for your store** carousel showcases complementary Shopify apps. Swipe or wait for the carousel to auto-rotate to discover apps that pair well with Avada PDF Invoice.

{/* screenshot: Recommended apps carousel showing app cards with descriptions and CTAs */}

### Step 7: Change Your App Language

In the top-right area of the dashboard, use the **locale selector** dropdown to change the language used within the app interface.

{/* screenshot: Top-right corner of the dashboard showing the locale selector dropdown */}

### Step 8: Check Notifications

Click the **notification bell** icon in the header area to see the latest news and updates about the app.

{/* screenshot: Notification bell icon with the notification panel expanded */}

## Tips

- Complete all four Quickstart tasks to get the most out of Avada PDF Invoice right away.
- You can collapse or expand the Quickstart guide by clicking the chevron arrow next to the close button.
- The Quickstart guide can be dismissed by clicking the **X** button if you have already completed setup.
