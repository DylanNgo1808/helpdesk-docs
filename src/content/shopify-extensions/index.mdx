# Shopify Extensions

Access Avada PDF Invoice features right from your Shopify Admin. This section shows you how to use invoice actions on individual orders, in bulk, from draft orders, and at your point of sale.

## Guides

- [Order Page Actions](/docs/shopify-extensions/order-page-actions) — Use PDF invoice actions from a single order page
- [Order List Bulk Actions](/docs/shopify-extensions/order-list-actions) — Run invoice actions on multiple orders at once
- [Draft Order Actions](/docs/shopify-extensions/draft-order-actions) — Generate invoices for draft orders in Shopify
- [POS Extension](/docs/shopify-extensions/pos-extension) — Use PDF invoice features at your point of sale
