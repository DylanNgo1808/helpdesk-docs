# How to Send a Test Email

Before relying on email automation for your live orders, you can send a test email to verify that your configuration is correct. The test email uses your current automation settings and sends a sample invoice to the email address you specify.

## Prerequisites

- Better PDF Invoice app installed on your Shopify store
- Automation emails enabled with all required fields configured
- A valid **Reply to** email address, **PDF attached template**, and **Send email when** trigger configured
- At least one order in your store (the test email uses sample order data)

## Steps

### Step 1: Navigate to the Automation Emails settings

In the Better PDF Invoice app, click **Emails** in the left navigation menu and select the **Automation Emails** tab.

{/* screenshot: Automation Emails tab selected on the Emails page */}

### Step 2: Verify your automation settings are complete

Before sending a test email, make sure all required fields are filled in: the **Custom PDF file name**, **PDF attached template**, **Send email when** trigger, and **Reply to** address. The Send test button is disabled if automation is not enabled.

{/* screenshot: Completed automation email form with all required fields filled in */}

### Step 3: Click the Send Test button

At the bottom of the Automation Emails page, click the **Send Test** button (with the send icon). This button only appears when the **Send automation email** toggle is enabled. If any required fields are missing, the form displays validation errors instead of opening the modal.

{/* screenshot: Send Test button at the bottom of the automation emails page */}

### Step 4: Enter the test recipient email

A modal opens with the title "Send test email." Enter the email address where you want to receive the test email in the **Email** field (e.g., "username@example.com").

{/* screenshot: Send test email modal with the email input field */}

### Step 5: Send the test email

Click the **Send email** button in the modal. The button shows a loading state while the email is being sent.

{/* screenshot: Send email button in the modal with loading state */}

### Step 6: Confirm the test email was sent

After the email is sent successfully, the modal closes and a success toast notification appears with the message confirming the email was sent. If the send fails, an error message is displayed.

{/* screenshot: Success toast notification after sending the test email */}

### Step 7: Check your inbox

Open the email inbox for the address you entered. Look for the test invoice email. Verify the following:

- The email arrives in your inbox (check spam/junk if needed)
- The reply-to address is correct
- The PDF attachment or online view link is included (based on your Document access setting)
- The PDF file name matches your custom file name configuration
- The invoice template renders correctly

{/* screenshot: Test email received in inbox showing the invoice PDF attachment */}

## Tips

- The test email uses your current unsaved settings. Save your settings first if you want the test to reflect your latest configuration.
- If the **Send Test** button is disabled, make sure the **Send automation email** toggle is turned on.
- Validation runs before the modal opens. If you see validation errors on the form fields (red error messages), fix those issues first before attempting to send a test.
- Test emails do not appear in the email activity history table since they are not triggered by a real order event.
- If you do not receive the test email, check your spam folder and verify that the email address you entered is correct.
