# How to Resend Failed or Past Emails

If an automated email failed to send or was canceled, you can resend it directly from the email activity history table. The resend feature re-triggers the email for the same order and event, using your current automation settings.

## Prerequisites

- Better PDF Invoice app installed on your Shopify store
- A Professional or Ultimate plan (resending is not available on the Free plan)
- Automation emails enabled with completed configuration
- At least one email record in the activity history with a **Failed** or **Canceled** status

## Steps

### Step 1: Navigate to the email activity history

In the Better PDF Invoice app, go to **Emails** and select the **Automation Emails** tab. Scroll down to the **Activity History** section and expand it by clicking the header.

{/* screenshot: Activity History section expanded showing email records */}

### Step 2: Identify failed or canceled emails

Look for rows in the history table with a red **Failed** badge or a yellow **Canceled** badge in the Status column. Hover over the badge to see the reason for failure or cancellation.

{/* screenshot: Failed and Canceled status badges with tooltip showing error details */}

### Step 3: Click the Resend button

In the **Actions** column of the row you want to resend, click the **Resend** button (send icon). The button shows a loading spinner while the email is being resent.

{/* screenshot: Resend button (send icon) in the Actions column of a failed email row */}

### Step 4: Wait for the resend to complete

The resend button displays a loading state while the app processes the request. The email is sent synchronously, so the result is available immediately.

{/* screenshot: Resend button in loading state */}

### Step 5: Confirm the resend result

After the resend completes:

- **Success**: A success toast notification appears with the message confirming the email was resent. The table refreshes automatically and the row status updates.
- **Failure**: An error toast notification appears with the failure message. The row status remains as Failed.

{/* screenshot: Success toast notification after a successful resend */}

### Step 6: Verify the updated status

After the table refreshes, check the status badge for the row you resent. A successful resend updates the status to **Succeed** (green badge). If it failed again, the status remains **Failed**.

{/* screenshot: Updated row showing Succeed status after a successful resend */}

### Step 7: Investigate persistent failures

If a resend continues to fail, hover over the **Failed** badge to read the error message. Common causes include:

- Invalid or missing customer email address
- Email service temporary errors
- Template configuration issues

Address the root cause before attempting to resend again.

{/* screenshot: Failed badge tooltip showing a specific error message */}

## Tips

- The **Resend** button is disabled for emails with a **Succeed** status. You can only resend failed or canceled emails.
- The **Resend** button is also disabled on the Free plan. Upgrade to Professional or Ultimate to use this feature.
- Resending uses your current automation email settings (template, reply-to, BCC, etc.), not the settings that were active when the original email was sent.
- The activity history table refreshes automatically after a resend, so you do not need to manually reload the page.
- If you need to send an invoice for an order that is not in the history, use the manual send feature from the order detail page instead.
