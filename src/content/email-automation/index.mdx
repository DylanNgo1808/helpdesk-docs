Automate your invoice delivery so customers receive PDF invoices without any manual work. This section covers setting up automation rules, customizing email templates, configuring sender details, and tracking delivery history.

- [Viewing Email Activity History](/docs/email-automation/email-history) — Track sent emails and their delivery status
