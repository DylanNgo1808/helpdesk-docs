# How to Set Up Your Email Sender Address

Avada PDF Invoice allows you to configure email addresses for receiving bulk PDF files. This guide shows you how to set up the recipient email and BCC addresses for the "Send Bulk PDF file to email" feature.

## Prerequisites

- Avada PDF Invoice installed on your Shopify store
- A valid email address for receiving bulk PDF exports

## Steps

### Step 1: Open Settings

From the Avada PDF Invoice app, click **Settings** in the app navigation.

{/* screenshot: Avada PDF Invoice navigation with "Settings" highlighted */}

### Step 2: Navigate to Send Bulk PDF File to Email

In the left sidebar, click **Send Bulk PDF file to email**. This section is identified by a send/mail icon.

{/* screenshot: Settings sidebar with "Send Bulk PDF file to email" highlighted */}

### Step 3: Review the Section Description

At the top of the panel, you will see the heading **Send Bulk PDF file to email** with the description: "You will receive the exported file at the recipient and BCC email addresses below." This confirms you are in the correct section.

{/* screenshot: The "Send Bulk PDF file to email" section header with the description text */}

### Step 4: Enter the Recipient Email

In the **Recipient** field, enter the primary email address where you want bulk PDF exports to be delivered (e.g., "username@example.com"). This is the main email that will receive the exported PDF files.

{/* screenshot: The "Recipient" text field with placeholder "username@example.com" */}

### Step 5: Add BCC Email Addresses

In the **BCC** field, enter any additional email addresses that should receive a copy of the bulk PDF export. You can enter multiple email addresses separated by commas.

{/* screenshot: The "BCC" text field with placeholder "username@example.com" and help text visible */}

### Step 6: Review BCC Limits

Note the help text below the BCC field: email addresses must be separated by commas, and "PDF will be delivered as BCC to the above email list." You can add up to 5 BCC email addresses.

{/* screenshot: The BCC field help text showing "Separated by comma(,)." and "PDF will be delivered as BCC to the above email list" */}

### Step 7: Save Your Changes

Click the **Save** button in the save bar at the top of the page. A "Save successfully" toast message will confirm your email settings have been saved.

{/* screenshot: The save bar at the top with "Save" button and the success toast message */}

### Step 8: Test Your Email Settings

To verify your email configuration works, navigate to the **Orders** page, select multiple orders, and use the bulk send feature. The PDF files will be sent to the recipient and BCC addresses you configured.

{/* screenshot: Orders page with multiple orders selected and the bulk send action available */}

## Tips

- Make sure to enter valid email addresses. The app will validate the recipient email format when you save.
- The BCC field accepts up to 5 email addresses. If you need to send to more recipients, consider using an email distribution list.
- The recipient email is separate from the contact email in Store details. The recipient email is specifically for receiving bulk PDF exports.
- If you receive a validation error, check that your email addresses are correctly formatted and separated by commas in the BCC field.
