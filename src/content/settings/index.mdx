# Settings

Customize Avada PDF Invoice to match your brand and business needs. This section covers every configurable option, from store details and logo to language preferences and payment terms.

## Guides

- [Store Details](/docs/settings/store-details) — Set your store name, address, and contact info
- [Upload Logo](/docs/settings/upload-logo) — Add your brand logo to invoices
- [Email Sender](/docs/settings/email-sender) — Configure the sender name and email address
- [Language Settings](/docs/settings/language-settings) — Choose the default language for your invoices
- [Payment Terms](/docs/settings/payment-terms) — Define payment terms displayed on invoices
- [Theme Selection](/docs/settings/theme-selection) — Pick a visual theme for your invoice layout
