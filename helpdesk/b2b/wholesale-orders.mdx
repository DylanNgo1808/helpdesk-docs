# How to Manage Wholesale Orders

Wholesale orders in Better PDF Invoice provide advanced B2B order management features including order combining, financial tracking, and bulk operations. This guide covers the key workflows for handling wholesale orders.

## Prerequisites

- A Wholesale plan in Better PDF Invoice
- A Shopify Plus store with B2B features enabled
- B2B access scopes granted to the app
- Orders associated with B2B companies

## Steps

### Step 1: Access B2B Orders

Navigate to **B2B** in the sidebar and click on a company to view its wholesale orders. The order table shows all orders from that company.

{/* screenshot: Company detail page with wholesale orders */}

### Step 2: Review Financial Statistics

The statistics bar at the top of the page provides an at-a-glance financial overview:
- **Total order value** - sum of all order values
- **Total amount paid** - total payments received
- **Total amount owed** - outstanding balance across all orders
- **Total amount past due** - overdue amount

{/* screenshot: Statistics bar with financial metrics */}

### Step 3: Filter by Payment Status

Use the status tabs to quickly filter orders by payment status. Select **Unpaid** to see orders that still require payment, which is useful for managing outstanding invoices.

{/* screenshot: Status tabs with Unpaid filter selected */}

### Step 4: Select Orders to Combine

Check the boxes next to 2-10 unpaid orders that you want to combine into a single invoice. The **Combine orders** button appears in the bulk action bar.

{/* screenshot: Multiple orders selected with Combine orders button visible */}

### Step 5: Validate Order Selection

The app validates your selection before combining. You cannot combine orders that are:
- **Closed** - closed orders cannot be combined
- **Paid** - only unpaid orders can be combined
- **Without payment terms** - orders must have payment terms set

If invalid orders are selected, a tooltip message explains why combining is disabled.

{/* screenshot: Tooltip showing validation message on disabled Combine orders button */}

### Step 6: Combine the Orders

Click **Combine orders** to proceed. You will be redirected to the combine orders page where you can review the combined invoice before finalizing.

{/* screenshot: Combine orders page with selected orders listed */}

### Step 7: Download Historical Documents

For bulk document downloads, use the **Download all** option from the bulk action bar. This downloads PDF invoices for all orders matching your current filters.

{/* screenshot: Download all button in bulk action bar */}

### Step 8: Print Wholesale Orders

Select orders and click **Print** in the bulk action bar to open the print preview for all selected orders at once.

{/* screenshot: Print preview for multiple wholesale orders */}

### Step 9: Download a B2B Statement

Click the **Download statement** button to generate a combined statement PDF for the company. See [Creating B2B Statements](/helpdesk/b2b/b2b-statements) for detailed instructions.

{/* screenshot: Download statement button */}

## Tips

- You can combine a minimum of 2 and a maximum of 10 orders at a time.
- The Wholesale plan is required for B2B features. The plan is available exclusively for Shopify Plus stores.
- Wholesale plan features include company management, balance owing calculation, credit limits, historical documents, net terms, wholesale templates, and the ability to sum unpaid orders for one payment.
- Use the date range selector to focus on a specific time period when reviewing financial statistics.
