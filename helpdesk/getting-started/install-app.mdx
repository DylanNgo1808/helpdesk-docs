# How to Install Better PDF Invoice

This guide walks you through installing the Better PDF Invoice app on your Shopify store so you can start generating, printing, and sending professional PDF invoices.

## Prerequisites

- An active Shopify store with admin access
- A web browser logged in to your Shopify admin dashboard

## Steps

### Step 1: Open the Shopify App Store

Go to the [Shopify App Store](https://apps.shopify.com/) in your browser, or navigate to **Apps** in your Shopify admin sidebar and click **Shopify App Store** at the bottom of the page.

{/* screenshot: Shopify admin sidebar with the "Apps" menu item highlighted and the "Shopify App Store" link visible */}

### Step 2: Search for Better PDF Invoice

In the App Store search bar, type **"Better PDF Invoice"** or **"AG Order Printer"** and press Enter.

{/* screenshot: Shopify App Store search bar with "Better PDF Invoice" typed in and search results showing */}

### Step 3: Select the App from Search Results

Click on the **Better PDF Invoice** app listing in the search results to open the app details page.

{/* screenshot: Search results page with the Better PDF Invoice app card highlighted */}

### Step 4: Click "Add app"

On the app listing page, click the **Add app** button to begin the installation process.

{/* screenshot: Better PDF Invoice app listing page with the "Add app" button highlighted */}

### Step 5: Review Permissions

Shopify will display the permissions the app requires to function, such as access to orders, products, and store data. Review these permissions carefully.

{/* screenshot: Shopify permissions review page showing the list of requested permissions */}

### Step 6: Click "Install app"

After reviewing the permissions, click the **Install app** button to confirm the installation.

{/* screenshot: The install confirmation page with the "Install app" button highlighted */}

### Step 7: Wait for Installation to Complete

The app will install and automatically redirect you to the Better PDF Invoice dashboard. You will see a welcome message that says **"Hi [Your Name]!"** along with the Quickstart guide.

{/* screenshot: Better PDF Invoice dashboard showing the welcome message and Quickstart guide */}

## Tips

- If you cannot find the app in the Shopify App Store, make sure you are searching with the correct name: "Better PDF Invoice" or "AG Order Printer."
- After installation, the app will appear under the **Apps** section in your Shopify admin sidebar for easy access.
- The app includes a free plan so you can explore its features before upgrading.
