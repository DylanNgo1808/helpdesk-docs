# How to Complete the Initial Setup

After installing Better PDF Invoice, you should complete the initial setup to ensure your invoices display the correct store information, logo, and branding. This guide walks you through each step.

## Prerequisites

- Better PDF Invoice installed on your Shopify store
- Your store information handy (company name, address, phone, email, VAT number)
- Your store logo file in an image format (PNG, JPG, or GIF)

## Steps

### Step 1: Open the Dashboard Quickstart

Navigate to the Better PDF Invoice app from your Shopify admin. On the dashboard, locate the **Quickstart** section and click the **Set up** button next to "Set up store's information and language."

{/* screenshot: Dashboard Quickstart section with the "Set up" button highlighted on the first task */}

### Step 2: Select Your Invoice Language

In the setup modal that appears, use the **Language on Invoices** dropdown to select the primary language for your invoice documents. This determines the language used for labels like "Invoice," "Subtotal," and "Grand Total."

{/* screenshot: Store details modal showing the "Language on Invoices" dropdown with language options */}

### Step 3: Enter Your Company Name

Fill in the **Company name** field with your business or store name (e.g., "Avada Commerce"). This name will appear on all your invoices.

{/* screenshot: Store details modal with the "Company name" field highlighted */}

### Step 4: Enter Your Store Address

Fill in the **Address** field with your business address (e.g., "445 Mount Eden Road, Mount Eden, Auckland"). This appears in the store information section of your invoices.

{/* screenshot: Store details modal with the "Address" field highlighted */}

### Step 5: Add Your VAT Number and Phone

Fill in the **VAT number** field with your tax identification number and the **Phone** field with your business phone number. These fields are optional but recommended for professional invoices.

{/* screenshot: Store details modal showing the "VAT number" and "Phone" fields side by side */}

### Step 6: Add Your Email and Registered Number

Enter your business **Email** address (e.g., "sales@domain.com") and your **Registered number** if applicable. The email will appear as your contact email on invoices.

{/* screenshot: Store details modal showing the "Email" and "Registered number" fields */}

### Step 7: Save Your Store Details

Click the **Save** button at the bottom of the modal to save your store information. A success toast will confirm your details have been saved, and the Quickstart task will be marked as completed.

{/* screenshot: Store details modal with the "Save" button highlighted and the success toast visible */}

### Step 8: Upload Your Store Logo

Navigate to **Settings** from the left sidebar, then go to the **Store details** tab. In the **Logo** section, click the drag-and-drop zone or click the **Upload logo** button to select your logo image file. Your logo will appear in the preview thumbnail.

{/* screenshot: Settings > Store details page showing the Logo section with the DropZone and Upload logo button */}

### Step 9: Adjust Logo Dimensions

After uploading, optionally adjust the **Logo height** and **Logo width** fields to control how your logo appears on PDF invoices. Default values are 75px height and 150px width. You can also set separate dimensions for email logos using the **Email logo height** and **Email logo width** fields.

{/* screenshot: Logo dimension fields showing "Logo height", "Logo width", "Email logo height", and "Email logo width" */}

### Step 10: Save Your Settings

Click the **Save** button in the top save bar to apply your logo and dimension changes.

{/* screenshot: The save bar at the top of the Settings page with the "Save" button highlighted */}

### Step 11: Select Your First Template

Return to the dashboard and click the **Select template** button in the Quickstart section. A modal will appear showing available PDF templates. Browse the templates and click to select your preferred template for invoices.

{/* screenshot: Template selection modal showing template cards with previews */}

### Step 12: Confirm Your Template Selection

After selecting a template, click the confirmation button to save your choice. The Quickstart task will be marked as completed.

{/* screenshot: Template selection modal with a template selected and the confirm button highlighted */}

## Tips

- You can return to **Settings > Store details** at any time to update your store information.
- If your logo looks too large or too small on invoices, adjust the logo height and width fields under Settings.
- Choose a template that matches your brand style. You can always change or customize it later from the **Templates** page.
- All four Quickstart tasks can be completed in any order, but we recommend starting with store details.
