# How to Create Your First Invoice in Under 5 Minutes

This guide takes you through the fastest path to producing your first PDF invoice, from selecting a template to printing or downloading it.

## Prerequisites

- Better PDF Invoice installed on your Shopify store
- At least one order in your Shopify store (from the past 3 months)
- Store details configured (company name, address -- see [Initial Setup](/helpdesk/getting-started/initial-setup))

## Steps

### Step 1: Open the App Dashboard

From your Shopify admin, click **Apps** in the sidebar and select **Better PDF Invoice**. You will land on the dashboard with the Quickstart guide visible.

{/* screenshot: Shopify admin sidebar with Better PDF Invoice selected, showing the dashboard */}

### Step 2: Set Up Store Details (If Not Done)

If the first Quickstart task "Set up store's information and language" is not yet completed, click the **Set up** button. Fill in your **Company name**, **Address**, **Email**, and select your **Language on Invoices** from the dropdown. Click **Save** to continue.

{/* screenshot: Store details modal with fields filled in and the Save button highlighted */}

### Step 3: Select a PDF Template

Click the **Select template** button on the second Quickstart task. A modal will display available templates organized by type (Invoice, Order, Packing Slip, Refund). Browse the templates and click to select the ones you want to use. Click the confirm button to save your selection.

{/* screenshot: Template selection modal showing available invoice templates with select checkboxes */}

### Step 4: Customize Your Template (Optional)

Click the **Customize template** button on the third Quickstart task. Select which template you want to customize. This opens the template editor where you can adjust colors, fonts, layout, and the information displayed on your invoice.

{/* screenshot: Customize template modal showing template options to edit */}

### Step 5: Print Your First Invoice

On the fourth Quickstart task "Print/Download your first Invoice," click the **Print** button. The app will load your most recent order and open it in the print view using your selected template.

{/* screenshot: Quickstart section with the "Print" button highlighted on the fourth task */}

### Step 6: Review the Print Preview

The print preview page displays your invoice with all the store details, order information, and line items. Review the invoice to make sure everything looks correct -- your logo, company name, address, and order details should all be visible.

{/* screenshot: Print preview page showing a fully rendered PDF invoice with store details and order items */}

### Step 7: Print or Save as PDF

Use your browser's print dialog to print the invoice directly or save it as a PDF file. Click **Print** in the browser dialog or choose **Save as PDF** as the destination.

{/* screenshot: Browser print dialog with "Save as PDF" option selected */}

### Step 8: Download Your Invoice (Alternative)

Instead of printing, you can click the **Download** button on the fourth Quickstart task to directly download the invoice as a PDF file. The file will save to your computer's downloads folder.

{/* screenshot: Quickstart section with the "Download" button highlighted on the fourth task */}

### Step 9: Verify the Downloaded File

Open the downloaded PDF file to verify that all information appears correctly. The file name will follow the format configured in your settings.

{/* screenshot: A downloaded PDF invoice file opened in a PDF viewer */}

### Step 10: Explore the Orders Page

Now that you have created your first invoice, navigate to the **Orders** page from the app sidebar to view all your orders. From here you can print, download, or send invoices for any order.

{/* screenshot: Orders page showing a list of orders with action buttons for print, download, and send */}

## Tips

- If you see the message "Your store does not have any orders yet," you need to create a test order in Shopify first.
- If you see "Your store does not have any orders from the past 3 months," check that you have recent orders available.
- You can always change your template later from the **Templates** page without losing any data.
- After the Quickstart is complete, you can dismiss it by clicking the **X** button on the Quickstart card.
