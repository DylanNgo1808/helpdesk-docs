# Better PDF Invoice Help Center

Welcome to the Better PDF Invoice documentation. Here you will find step-by-step guides for every feature of the app, from initial setup to advanced customization.

## Documentation Sections

### [Getting Started](/helpdesk/getting-started)
Install the app, configure your store, and generate your first PDF invoice. This section covers everything you need to get up and running quickly.

### [Settings](/helpdesk/settings)
Configure your store details, invoice numbering, tax settings, payment methods, and other global preferences that apply to all invoices.

### [Templates](/helpdesk/templates)
Browse, create, and customize invoice templates. Learn how to edit the layout, theme colors, fonts, and content of your PDF invoices.

### [Orders](/helpdesk/orders)
View, search, filter, print, download, and manage invoices for your Shopify orders. Learn about single and bulk order operations.

### [Draft Orders](/helpdesk/draft-orders)
Generate PDF invoices for draft orders, including printing, downloading, and managing draft order documents.

### [Email Automation](/helpdesk/email-automation)
Set up automated email delivery of invoices to customers. Configure triggers, email templates, and scheduling.

### [Delivery Methods](/helpdesk/delivery-methods)
Configure external delivery methods for your invoices, including FTP, SFTP, and Google Drive integrations.

### [B2B Features](/helpdesk/b2b)
Manage company-specific invoice settings, tax exemptions, and custom templates for B2B customers.

### [Subscription](/helpdesk/subscription)
View and manage your Better PDF Invoice subscription plan. Compare plan features and upgrade or downgrade your account.

### [Shopify Extensions](/helpdesk/shopify-extensions)
Use Better PDF Invoice actions directly from Shopify Admin pages. Print, download, and send invoices from order detail pages, the order list, draft orders, and Shopify POS.

### [Troubleshooting](/helpdesk/troubleshooting)
Resolve common issues with invoice generation, email delivery, template rendering, connection errors, and more.
