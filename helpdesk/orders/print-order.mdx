# How to Print a Single Order Invoice

The print page lets you preview an order invoice, select which templates to include, choose a language, and print or download the PDF.

## Prerequisites

- Better PDF Invoice app installed on your Shopify store
- At least one order synced from your Shopify store
- At least one invoice template configured with the template type "Order"

## Steps

### Step 1: Navigate to the Print Page

From the **Orders** page, click the **Print** icon on an order row, or open an order detail and click **Print** in the secondary actions bar. The print page opens with the title "Print order."

{/* screenshot: Print order page with title and preview area */}

### Step 2: Wait for the Preview to Generate

The page displays a "Generating..." spinner while the PDF preview loads. Once ready, the invoice preview appears in the main content area.

{/* screenshot: Generating spinner followed by the rendered PDF preview */}

### Step 3: Review the PDF Preview

The left side of the page shows an embedded PDF preview of your invoice. Scroll through the preview to review all pages of the generated document.

{/* screenshot: Embedded PDF preview of the invoice in the main content area */}

### Step 4: Select Invoice Templates

On the right sidebar, under **Templates** with the description "Select templates to print your orders," check or uncheck templates from the list. Only templates with the type "Order" are shown. Multiple templates can be selected to combine them into one document.

{/* screenshot: Template selection panel with checkboxes for Order-type templates */}

### Step 5: Choose a Language

Use the **Choose language** dropdown above the template list to select the language for your invoice. The dropdown lists all available languages.

{/* screenshot: Choose language dropdown with language options */}

### Step 6: Download the Invoice

Click the **Download** primary action button in the top-right corner to download the invoice as a PDF file to your device.

{/* screenshot: Download button in the page header */}

### Step 7: View the Order in Shopify

Click the **View** button with the external link icon in the secondary actions to open the original order in your Shopify admin.

{/* screenshot: View button in secondary actions bar */}

### Step 8: Manage Templates

At the bottom of the template sidebar, click **View all** to go to the templates management page, or click **Add template** to create a new template.

{/* screenshot: View all and Add template buttons at the bottom of the template panel */}

### Step 9: Navigate Back

Click the back arrow in the top-left corner to return to the previous page.

{/* screenshot: Back arrow button in the top-left corner */}

## Tips

- Templates marked as the default print template are automatically selected when the page loads.
- If your browser does not support embedded PDF viewing, the invoice will automatically download instead of showing a preview.
- The print preview updates automatically when you change the selected templates or language.
- The language option may require a Professional plan or higher depending on your subscription.
