# How to Configure Payment Terms and Invoice Notes

Better PDF Invoice allows you to customize the notes, thank-you messages, and footer text that appear on your invoices. This guide shows you how to configure these text elements through the Translation settings so your invoices communicate your payment terms and business messages clearly.

## Prerequisites

- Better PDF Invoice installed on your Shopify store
- At least one template created or selected

## Steps

### Step 1: Open Settings

From the Better PDF Invoice app, click **Settings** in the app navigation.

{/* screenshot: Better PDF Invoice navigation with "Settings" highlighted */}

### Step 2: Navigate to Translation

In the left sidebar, click **Translation**. The translation settings control all text labels and messages that appear on your invoices, including notes and footer content.

{/* screenshot: Settings sidebar with "Translation" highlighted */}

### Step 3: Wait for Translation Data to Load

The translation settings will load your current language configuration. Wait until the translation labels appear in the editing area.

{/* screenshot: Translation page fully loaded with the Advanced settings and Translation sections visible */}

### Step 4: Select the General Tab

Click the **General** tab in the translation section. This tab contains the common labels used across all document types, including notes and thank-you messages.

{/* screenshot: Translation tabs with "General" tab selected */}

### Step 5: Find the Notes Field

Scroll down or use the **Search translation** field to locate the **Notes** label. This field controls the notes section text that appears on your invoices. Edit this field to add your payment terms, return policy, or any other important notes.

{/* screenshot: The "Notes" translation field with editable text */}

### Step 6: Edit the Thank-You Message

Locate the **Thank you for your business** field (labeled as "Thank notes" in the translation list). Edit this field to customize the thank-you message that appears at the bottom of your invoices.

{/* screenshot: The "Thank notes" translation field showing "Thank you for your business" */}

### Step 7: Customize the Footer

Locate the **Footer** field in the translation list. This text appears at the very bottom of your invoice documents. Use this for legal disclaimers, payment instructions, or additional business information.

{/* screenshot: The "Footer" translation field with editable text */}

### Step 8: Edit Net Payment Terms

Locate the **Net payment** field. This is commonly used to display payment terms like "Net 30," "Net 60," or "Due upon receipt." Edit the label to match your preferred payment terms text.

{/* screenshot: The "Net payment" translation field showing the current value */}

### Step 9: Customize the Email Tab Messages

Click the **Email** tab to configure email-specific text. Here you can edit:

- **Email subject** -- The subject line for invoice emails
- **Custom message** -- A personalized message in the email body
- **Best regards** -- The sign-off text
- **Email signature** -- Your business signature line
- **Download button text** -- The text on the PDF download button in emails

{/* screenshot: The Email tab showing fields for email subject, custom message, best regards, and email signature */}

### Step 10: Set Draft Order Email Text

Still in the **Email** tab, scroll to find draft order-specific fields:

- **Email subject draft** -- Subject line for draft order/quote emails
- **Please find draft** -- The introductory text for draft order emails

Edit these to reflect your quotation or proforma invoice terms.

{/* screenshot: The Email tab showing draft order email fields */}

### Step 11: Save Your Changes

Click the **Save** button in the save bar at the top of the page. A "Save successfully" toast will confirm your payment terms and notes have been updated.

{/* screenshot: The save bar with "Save" button highlighted and the success toast visible */}

### Step 12: Preview Your Changes

Navigate to **Orders**, select an order, and preview or print the invoice to verify your notes, thank-you message, and footer text appear correctly.

{/* screenshot: An invoice preview showing the custom notes and thank-you message at the bottom of the document */}

## Tips

- Payment terms in the Notes field are visible to your customers, so write them clearly and professionally.
- You can use different notes for different languages by switching the language in the translation settings and editing the Notes field for each language.
- The Footer field is a good place for legal disclaimers or bank account details for wire transfers.
- Changes to translation labels apply to all new invoices. Previously generated PDFs are not affected.
- If you have multiple templates, the notes and footer text apply globally to all templates that include these sections.
