# Helpdesk Article Standard Format

Every article MUST follow this MDX format exactly:

```mdx
---
title: "How to [Action] in Better PDF Invoice"
description: "Learn how to [action] [context] in the Better PDF Invoice app."
---

# How to [Action]

[1-2 sentence overview of what this guide covers and why you'd want to do it.]

## Prerequisites

- [What the user needs before starting, e.g., "You must have at least one order in your Shopify store"]

## Steps

### Step 1: [Action verb] [Object]

[1-2 sentences explaining this step.]

{/* screenshot: [Description of what the screenshot would show] */}

### Step 2: [Action verb] [Object]

[1-2 sentences explaining this step.]

{/* screenshot: [Description of what the screenshot would show] */}

[Continue for all steps...]

## Tips

- [Helpful tip 1]
- [Helpful tip 2]

## Related Articles

- [Link to related article 1](/helpdesk/section/article)
- [Link to related article 2](/helpdesk/section/article)
```

## Rules:
1. Title format: "How to [Verb]..." — task-oriented
2. 5-12 steps per article (not too few, not too many)
3. Every step has a screenshot callout comment
4. Always include Prerequisites, Tips, and Related Articles
5. Use simple language — merchant audience, not developers
6. Mention specific UI elements by name (buttons, tabs, fields)
7. Each _meta.json file orders pages logically
