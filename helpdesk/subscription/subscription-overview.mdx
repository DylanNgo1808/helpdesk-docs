# How to Understand Your Subscription Plan

Better PDF Invoice offers multiple subscription tiers, each with different features and limits. This guide explains the available plans and how to view your current subscription details.

## Prerequisites

- An active Better PDF Invoice installation on your Shopify store

## Steps

### Step 1: Navigate to the Subscription Page

From the Better PDF Invoice dashboard, click **Subscription** in the left sidebar menu. This opens the subscription management page.

{/* screenshot: Left sidebar with Subscription menu item highlighted */}

### Step 2: Review the Billing Interval Toggle

At the top of the page, use the **Monthly** and **Annually** toggle buttons to switch between billing intervals. Choosing annually saves you 18% compared to monthly billing.

{/* screenshot: Monthly and Annually toggle buttons with Save 18% badge */}

### Step 3: Compare Available Plans

The pricing table displays plan cards side by side. Each plan includes:

**Professional** plan:
- Tax exemptions
- POS integration
- Custom email sender
- Customizable templates
- Credit and delivery notes
- Print and export orders in bulk
- Remove brand mark from invoice
- Unlimited orders and draft orders per month
- Print and export with multiple languages

**Ultimate** plan (includes all Professional features plus):
- Multiple currencies
- High priority support
- Remove brand mark from email
- Download invoice by billing address
- Expert service for template customization

**Wholesale** plan (includes all Ultimate features plus):
- Company management
- Balance owing calculation
- Credit limits for store credit
- Historical documents
- Net terms
- Wholesale templates
- Sum unpaid orders for one payment

{/* screenshot: Pricing table showing Professional, Ultimate, and Wholesale plan cards */}

### Step 4: View the Free Plan

Click the **Show all plans** button below the pricing table to reveal the Free plan details. The Free plan includes:
- Basic themes
- 50 orders per month
- Automation email
- 50 draft orders per month
- Print and export orders

{/* screenshot: Show all plans button and Free plan card */}

### Step 5: View Your Current Plan Details

Scroll down to the **Plan details** section at the bottom of the page. This section displays:
- Your **shop owner name** and avatar
- **Member since** date - when your subscription started
- **Next payment** date and amount (for paid plans)
- **Original amount** if a discount is applied

{/* screenshot: Plan details section showing member info and payment details */}

### Step 6: Check Your Current Plan Status

Your active plan is indicated by a **Current plan** label on the corresponding plan card. The button is disabled for your current plan.

{/* screenshot: Plan card with Current plan label */}

### Step 7: Review the FAQ Section

Scroll further down to find the **Frequently asked questions** section covering common billing topics like cancellation, refunds, trial periods, and plan switching.

{/* screenshot: FAQ section with expandable questions */}

## Tips

- The Wholesale plan requires a Shopify Plus store. If you are not on Shopify Plus, clicking the Wholesale plan button will prompt you to upgrade your Shopify plan first.
- Paid plans include a trial period (7 days for Professional and Ultimate) for new users.
- You can cancel your subscription at any time. Shopify handles prorated charges when switching between plans.
- The app includes a 30-day Money Back Guarantee for the first month of a paid subscription.
- The "Cancel at anytime. 100% Risk free" notice is displayed on the subscription page for your assurance.
